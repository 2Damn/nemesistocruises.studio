Nemesis To Cruises Records

Nemesis To Cruises Records is an independent Bangladeshi record label & artist management service. Nemesis To Cruises Records (N2C) has been supporting all genre music internationally. It is the home of talented and rising artists.

Founded By Prayangshu, Co-Founded By Arfat Faisal & Ranit Roy
